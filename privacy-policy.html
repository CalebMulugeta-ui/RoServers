RoServers — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

RoServers is a browser extension that lets you choose which Roblox game server you join, based on its region and estimated ping. This policy explains exactly what data the extension accesses and how it is used. In plain terms: RoServers does not collect, store on any server, sell, or share your personal information. Everything it does happens locally in your browser.

What the extension accesses

RoServers makes network requests only to provide its core features:

  • Roblox public APIs (games.roblox.com, gamejoin.roblox.com, thumbnails.roblox.com) — to read the list of public servers for the game you are viewing, determine each server's region, and show player avatars. These are the same public endpoints the Roblox website itself uses.
  • Approximate location (ipapi.co) — to estimate your ping to each server region, the extension performs a one-time lookup of your approximate location based on your public IP address. Only your coordinates (and city/country labels) are read; this is used purely to rank regions by distance. The result is cached locally and refreshed at most once a week.

What is stored, and where

RoServers uses your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) only. The following is stored on your own device and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your approximate location result, to avoid repeated lookups.
  • A cache mapping Roblox server IDs to their region, so the extension does not re-query the same servers.
  • Your pinned (favorite) regions.
  • A flag noting that you have seen the welcome screen.

None of this data leaves your device. There is no RoServers account, no RoServers server, and no analytics or tracking.

What RoServers does NOT do

  • It does not collect or store your name, email, Roblox account, or any personally identifying information.
  • It does not sell or share any data with third parties.
  • It does not track your browsing across websites. It runs only on Roblox game pages.
  • It does not run any advertising or third-party trackers.

Third-party services

The only third-party service contacted is ipapi.co, used solely for approximate IP-based location to estimate ping. Roblox endpoints are contacted to read public game/server data. RoServers is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation.

Data removal

You can clear all locally stored data at any time from the extension's toolbar popup ("Clear region cache"), or by removing the extension, which deletes all of its local storage.
